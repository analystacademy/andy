Notice at Collection

Analyst Academy LLC — Effective March 31, 2026

California This Notice at Collection is provided to California residents under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), as amended by the California Privacy Rights Act (CPRA). It describes the categories of personal information we collect, the purposes for collection, and your rights regarding that information.

For our full Privacy Policy, visit Privacy Policy. To opt out of sale/sharing, visit Privacy Choices.

Categories We Collect

We collect the following categories of personal information:

Identifiers Collected

Name, email address, IP address, account ID, cookie identifiers, device identifiers.

Commercial Information Collected

Purchase history, transaction records, billing amounts, refund history.

Course & Learning Data Collected

Course enrollment, lesson progress, quiz results, completion records, and certificates.

Internet & Network Activity Collected

Pages visited, links clicked, videos watched, time on site, referral source, browser and device type.

Inferences Collected

Profiles derived from the above data, used for course recommendations, personalization, and marketing.

Sensitive Personal Information Not Collected

We do not collect Social Security numbers, financial account credentials, precise geolocation, or other sensitive categories.

Sources of Information

We collect personal information from the following sources:

  • Directly from you — when you create an account, purchase a course, register for live training, complete forms, or contact support
  • Automatically — through cookies, pixels, and tracking technologies when you visit our websites
  • Service providers:
    • Thinkific — course enrollment, progress, and completion data
    • Stripe & PayPal — payment confirmation and transaction data
    • Google Analytics 4 — aggregated and pseudonymous usage statistics
    • Hotjar — session recordings and heatmap data

Purposes of Collection

We collect and use personal information for the following business and commercial purposes:

  • Provide and operate the Services — account management, course delivery, payment processing, customer support
  • Analytics & security — understanding platform usage, preventing fraud, maintaining platform integrity, and improving user experience
  • Marketing — where permitted, sending promotional content, course updates, and educational materials to users who have opted in or where legitimate interest applies
  • Legal compliance — meeting our obligations under applicable law, responding to legal process, and enforcing our Terms & Conditions

Sale, Sharing & Opt-Out

We do not sell personal information in exchange for money. However, we may "share" certain personal information (such as cookie identifiers and browsing activity) with third-party advertising platforms — including Meta, Google, and LinkedIn — for cross-context behavioral advertising. Under the CCPA/CPRA, this may constitute "sharing" even without monetary consideration.

Opt Out: You can opt out of the sale or sharing of your personal information for behavioral advertising by visiting our Privacy Choices page.

Global Privacy Control (GPC)

We honor the Global Privacy Control (GPC) signal. If your browser sends a GPC signal, we treat it as a valid opt-out of sale and sharing — no additional action is needed.

Retention Periods

We retain personal information only as long as necessary for the purposes described above:

  • Account & billing records: 7 years from last transaction
  • Support communications: 24 months from resolution
  • Marketing data: Until you unsubscribe or 24 months of inactivity
  • Google Analytics 4 data: 14 months
  • Hotjar data: 12 months
  • Course progress & completion data: Lifetime of your active account

After these periods expire, data is deleted or anonymized unless required by law to retain longer.

Your Rights

As a California resident, you have the following rights under the CCPA/CPRA:

  • Right to Know — request disclosure of the categories and specific pieces of personal information we have collected about you
  • Right to Delete — request deletion of your personal information
  • Right to Correct — request correction of inaccurate personal information
  • Right to Portability — receive your data in a portable, machine-readable format
  • Right to Opt Out — opt out of the sale or sharing of your personal information (see Privacy Choices)
  • Right to Non-Discrimination — we will not discriminate against you for exercising your rights

How to Submit a Request

Email: team@theanalystacademy.com
Subject line: "Privacy Request"
Include your name, email address, and a description of your request. We may need to verify your identity before fulfilling the request.

For full details on how we handle your data, please review our Privacy Policy.
