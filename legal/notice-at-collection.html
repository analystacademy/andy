Notice at Collection

This Notice at Collection is provided pursuant to the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) and the California Privacy Rights Act (CPRA). It describes the categories of personal information Analyst Academy LLC ("Analyst Academy") collects, the purposes for collection, and your rights as a California consumer.

Categories of Personal Information We Collect

At or before the point of collection, we inform California residents of the following categories of personal information we collect and the purposes for which each category is used:

Identifiers

Examples: Name, email address, IP address, account username, billing address.

Purposes: Account creation, course enrollment, customer support, marketing communications, fraud prevention.

Sold or Shared: No.

Customer Records

Examples: Billing name, address, payment information (processed by Stripe or PayPal).

Purposes: Processing course purchases and live training payments, issuing refunds, maintaining purchase history.

Sold or Shared: No.

Commercial Information

Examples: Courses purchased, live training sessions booked, transaction history, refund history.

Purposes: Providing access to purchased content, customer support, and business analytics.

Sold or Shared: No.

Internet or Electronic Network Activity

Examples: Pages visited, links clicked, time on page, browser type, device type, IP address, referral URL, session recordings (via Hotjar).

Purposes: Website analytics, improving user experience, security monitoring.

Sold or Shared: No.

Education and Course Activity Data

Examples: Course enrollment, video lesson progress, lesson completion, quiz results, course completion percentage, certificates issued, live training registration and attendance.

Purposes: Delivering course content, tracking student progress, issuing completion certificates, supporting re-enrollment.

Sold or Shared: No.

Geolocation Data

Examples: Country and region inferred from IP address (non-precise).

Purposes: Analytics, content localization, fraud prevention.

Sold or Shared: No.

Inferences

Examples: Preferences and interests inferred from course enrollment and website activity.

Purposes: Personalized course recommendations and relevant marketing.

Sold or Shared: No.

Sensitive Personal Information

Analyst Academy does not intentionally collect sensitive personal information as defined by the CPRA (e.g., Social Security numbers, financial account credentials, precise geolocation, racial or ethnic origin, health data). If you believe we have collected such data, please contact us promptly.

Retention Periods

We retain each category of personal information for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes described above:

  • Identifiers and Customer Records: Duration of account plus 3 years.
  • Course Activity Data: 5 years to support certificate verification and re-enrollment.
  • Commercial Information / Payment Records: 7 years for tax and accounting compliance.
  • Internet Activity Data: Up to 14 months (Google Analytics 4) and 1 year (Hotjar).
  • Marketing Data: Until you unsubscribe or request deletion.

Service Providers & Processors

We share personal information with the following service providers, which process data only on our behalf and at our direction:

  • Thinkific — Course platform / LMS (account data, course enrollment, progress, completion).
  • Stripe — Payment processing (billing and payment data).
  • PayPal — Payment processing (billing and payment data).
  • Kit (formerly ConvertKit) — Email marketing (email address, name, engagement data).
  • Google Analytics 4 — Web analytics (anonymized usage and device data).
  • Hotjar — Experience analytics (anonymized session recordings, heatmaps).

Your California Privacy Rights

As a California resident, you have the right to:

  • Know what personal information we collect, use, disclose, or share.
  • Delete personal information we have collected about you (subject to certain exceptions).
  • Correct inaccurate personal information.
  • Opt Out of Sale or Sharing: We do not sell or share personal information for cross-context behavioral advertising.
  • Non-Discrimination: We will not discriminate against you for exercising your privacy rights.

To submit a request, email team@theanalystacademy.com with "CCPA Request" in the subject line. We will verify your identity and respond within 45 days (extendable by an additional 45 days with notice).
