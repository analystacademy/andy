Your Privacy Choices

Analyst Academy LLC ("Analyst Academy") respects your privacy and provides you with meaningful choices about how your personal information is used. This page explains the options available to you.

Opt Out of Marketing Emails

If you no longer wish to receive marketing emails or our free email course from Analyst Academy, you can unsubscribe at any time by:

  • Clicking the Unsubscribe link at the bottom of any marketing email we send.
  • Emailing us at team@theanalystacademy.com with "Unsubscribe" in the subject line.

Please note that even if you opt out of marketing emails, we may still send you transactional messages related to your account, course purchases, or live training registrations.

Cookie Preferences

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies on our website. You have the following options:

  • Browser Settings: Most browsers allow you to block or delete cookies through their settings. Please consult your browser's help documentation for instructions.
  • Google Analytics Opt-Out: You can prevent Google Analytics from collecting your data by installing the Google Analytics Opt-Out Browser Add-on.
  • Hotjar Opt-Out: You can prevent Hotjar from collecting data about you by visiting Hotjar's opt-out page.

Disabling certain cookies may affect the functionality of our website and your ability to access course content.

Do Not Sell or Share My Personal Information

Analyst Academy does not sell your personal information to third parties, nor do we share it for cross-context behavioral advertising. You do not need to take any action to opt out of these practices.

For more information about the data we collect and how we use it, please review our full Privacy Policy.

GDPR & CCPA Rights

Depending on your location, you may have additional rights regarding your personal data, including the right to access, correct, delete, or port your data. Please see our Privacy Policy for full details on your rights under the GDPR (EEA/UK residents) and CCPA (California residents).
