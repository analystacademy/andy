1. Scope

This Privacy Policy applies to all websites operated by Analyst Academy LLC, including theanalystacademy.com and courses.theanalystacademy.com (our course platform), as well as email communications, marketing messages, and other interactions you have with us. It does not apply to third-party websites linked from our sites.

By using our Services, you agree to the collection and use of information as described in this policy. If you do not agree, please discontinue use of our Services.

2. Information We Collect

Account & Profile Information

When you register for an account on our course platform (powered by Thinkific), we collect your name, email address, password (hashed), and any profile details you choose to provide.

Billing & Payment Information

Payments are processed by Stripe and PayPal via Thinkific. We do not store full card numbers or sensitive payment credentials. We retain order records, transaction IDs, and billing amounts for accounting and legal compliance.

Course Enrollment & Learning Data

We collect data about the courses you enroll in, your progress through lessons, quiz results, completion certificates, and other learning activity within the platform.

Live Training Registrations

When you register for "Presentation Storytelling" live team training, we collect your name, email, company/team details, session preferences, and any information you provide in registration forms.

Forms & Support Communications

If you contact us for support, submit a form, or respond to surveys, we collect the information you provide including your name, email, and message content.

Automatically Collected Data

When you visit our sites, we automatically collect:

  • IP address and approximate location (country/region)
  • Browser type, operating system, and device type
  • Pages visited, time spent, referral source, and click paths
  • Cookie identifiers and session data

From Service Providers

We may receive information about you from our service providers, such as Thinkific (course platform data), Stripe and PayPal (payment confirmation), and Google Analytics (aggregated usage statistics).

3. How We Use Information

We use information we collect for the following purposes and legal bases:

Providing our Services

Creating and managing your account, delivering course content, processing payments, and facilitating live training sessions. Legal basis: Contract performance.

Analytics & Improvement

Understanding how our platform is used, identifying issues, and improving course content and user experience. Legal basis: Legitimate interests.

Marketing & Communications

Sending you course updates, promotional offers, newsletters, and educational content where you have opted in or where permitted by law. Legal basis: Consent or legitimate interests.

Legal & Security

Detecting fraud, enforcing our Terms & Conditions, complying with applicable laws, and protecting the safety of our users and platform. Legal basis: Legal obligation / legitimate interests.

4. Cookies & Tracking

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies. You can manage your preferences via our Privacy Choices page.

Strictly Necessary

These cookies are required for the platform to function — authentication sessions, shopping cart, security tokens. They cannot be disabled.

Analytics Cookies

We use Google Analytics 4 (data retention: 14 months) and Hotjar (data retention: 12 months) to understand how visitors use our sites. These tools collect pseudonymous usage data including page views, scroll depth, and heatmaps. You may opt out via our Privacy Choices page or the Google Analytics opt-out browser add-on.

Advertising & Remarketing Cookies

We use advertising pixels and tags to measure campaign performance and show relevant ads to people who have visited our sites:

  • Meta Pixel — Facebook/Instagram advertising
  • Google Ads — Google advertising and conversion tracking
  • LinkedIn Insight Tag — LinkedIn advertising and analytics

You can opt out of behavioral advertising via our Privacy Choices page or through the Digital Advertising Alliance opt-out tool.

Tag Management

We use Google Tag Manager to deploy and manage tracking tags. Google Tag Manager itself does not collect personal data but may fire tags that do.

Video Hosting (Wistia)

Course videos are hosted via Wistia. When you watch an embedded video, Wistia may collect viewer analytics such as play rate, watch time, and device type. You can review Wistia's privacy policy for details on their data practices.

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information in the following circumstances:

Service Providers

We share data with vendors who help us operate the platform (see Section 16 for the full list). These vendors are contractually bound to use data only as directed by us.

Business Transfers

If Analyst Academy is involved in a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction. We will notify you via email and/or prominent notice on our site.

Legal Requirements

We may disclose information if required by law, court order, or government authority, or when we believe disclosure is necessary to protect our rights, prevent fraud, or ensure user safety.

With Your Consent

We may share information with third parties when you have given us explicit permission to do so.

6. International Transfers

Analyst Academy LLC is based in the United States. If you are accessing our Services from outside the U.S., your information will be transferred to and processed in the United States, where data protection laws may differ from those in your country.

For users in the European Economic Area (EEA) or United Kingdom, we rely on appropriate transfer mechanisms (such as Standard Contractual Clauses) when transferring personal data internationally. For more information, contact us at team@theanalystacademy.com.

7. Retention

We retain your information for as long as necessary to provide our Services and fulfill the purposes described in this policy. Specific retention periods:

  • Account & billing records: 7 years from last transaction (for tax and legal compliance)
  • Support communications: 24 months from resolution
  • Marketing data: Until you unsubscribe or 24 months of inactivity, whichever is first
  • Google Analytics 4 data: 14 months
  • Hotjar data: 12 months
  • Course progress & completion data: For the lifetime of your active account

After these periods, data is deleted or anonymized unless we are required by law to retain it longer.

8. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information, including:

  • HTTPS/TLS encryption for all data in transit
  • Hashed and salted password storage
  • Access controls and role-based permissions
  • Infrastructure hosted on AWS with Cloudflare DDoS/CDN protection

No method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your data, we cannot guarantee absolute security. If you believe your account has been compromised, please contact us immediately at team@theanalystacademy.com.

9. Your Rights & Choices

GDPR Rights (EEA & UK Residents)

If you are located in the EEA or UK, you have the right to:

  • Access — Request a copy of your personal data
  • Rectification — Correct inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Erasure — Request deletion of your personal data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Restriction — Request that we limit processing of your data
  • Portability — Receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format
  • Objection — Object to processing based on legitimate interests or for direct marketing
  • Withdraw Consent — Where processing is based on consent, withdraw it at any time

US State Privacy Rights

Residents of California, Virginia, Colorado, Connecticut, Texas, and other states with comprehensive privacy laws may have rights including access, deletion, correction, portability, and opt-out of sale/sharing. See Section 15 for California-specific disclosures.

How to Exercise Your Rights

Email us at team@theanalystacademy.com with the subject line "Privacy Request". Include your name, email address, and a description of your request. We will respond within 30 days (or as required by applicable law). We may need to verify your identity before fulfilling your request.
