Refund Policy

At Analyst Academy, we want you to be confident in your purchase. This Refund Policy explains when and how refunds are issued for our self-paced online courses and live team training programs.

1. Self-Paced Courses

We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee on all self-paced online courses, subject to the following conditions:

  • Eligible for a full refund: You request a refund within 30 days of purchase and you have completed less than 30% of the course.
  • Not eligible for a refund: You have completed 50% or more of the course, regardless of how many days have passed since purchase.
  • Between 30%–50% completion: Refund eligibility in this range is reviewed on a case-by-case basis. Please contact us to discuss your situation.

Course completion percentage is measured by lessons completed within the Thinkific platform.

Refunds are not issued for:

  • Requests made more than 30 days after the date of purchase.
  • Courses where 50% or more of the content has been accessed or completed.
  • Downloadable materials that have already been accessed.

2. Live Team Training

The following cancellation and refund terms apply to live team training sessions (including "Presentation Storytelling" and other live programs):

  • Cancellation 7 or more days before the scheduled session: Full refund of the amount paid.
  • Cancellation less than 7 days before the scheduled session: No refund will be issued. At our sole discretion, we may offer a credit toward a future session in lieu of a refund.
  • No-shows: Failure to attend a scheduled session without prior notice forfeits the session fee entirely.
  • Rescheduling: Requests to reschedule must be submitted at least 7 days in advance. We will make reasonable efforts to accommodate rescheduling requests, subject to availability.

3. How to Request a Refund

To request a refund, please email us at team@theanalystacademy.com with the following information:

  • Your full name
  • The email address associated with your account
  • The course or training session you are requesting a refund for
  • Your order number or date of purchase
  • A brief reason for your refund request (optional but helpful)

We will review your request and respond within 2 business days to confirm eligibility and next steps.

4. Chargebacks

We ask that you contact us at team@theanalystacademy.com before initiating a chargeback or payment dispute with your bank or credit card provider. In most cases, we can resolve issues quickly and directly.

Chargebacks initiated without prior contact with Analyst Academy may result in suspension of your account and loss of access to purchased courses while the dispute is under review.

5. Processing Time

Once a refund is approved, please allow 5–10 business days for the refund to appear in your account, depending on your payment method and financial institution. Refunds are returned to the original payment method used at the time of purchase (Stripe or PayPal).
