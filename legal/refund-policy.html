1) Self-Paced Courses

We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee on self-paced courses, subject to the following conditions:

  • Less than 30% completed: Full refund available within 30 days of purchase — no questions asked.
  • Between 30% and 50% completed: Refunds are at our discretion. Contact us and we'll do our best to work with you.
  • More than 50% completed: No refund is available. At this point, a substantial portion of the course content has been consumed.

Course completion percentage is tracked automatically via our course platform (Thinkific).

EU/EEA Customers: Under the EU Consumer Rights Directive, you have a statutory right to withdraw from a digital purchase within 14 days. By accessing course content before the 14-day period expires, you expressly consent to early delivery and acknowledge that your right of withdrawal is forfeited upon accessing the content. Our 30-day guarantee above is more generous than this statutory minimum where applicable.

Not sure if the course is right for you? Check out our free preview lessons and the course overview page before purchasing.

2) Live Training

Live training sessions (including "Presentation Storytelling" and other team workshops) are subject to the following cancellation terms:

  • 7 or more days before the session: Full refund.
  • Less than 7 days before the session: No refund. A credit toward a future session may be offered at our discretion.
  • No-shows: If you do not attend and have not cancelled, no refund or credit will be issued.

Rescheduling requests made 7 or more days in advance will be accommodated where possible.

3) How to Request a Refund

To request a refund, email us at team@theanalystacademy.com with the following information:

  • Your full name
  • Email address used at purchase
  • Order number or transaction ID
  • Course or session name
  • Reason for your refund request (optional but helpful)

We'll review your request and respond within 2 business days.

4) Processing Time

Approved refunds are processed within 5–10 business days. Refunds are returned to the original payment method (Stripe or PayPal). Depending on your bank or payment provider, it may take additional time for the funds to appear in your account.

5) Chargebacks

If you have a concern about a charge, please contact us before filing a chargeback with your bank or credit card company. We're happy to resolve issues directly and quickly. Chargebacks create friction and delays for both parties — we'd much rather just fix it.

Email us at team@theanalystacademy.com and we'll make it right.
